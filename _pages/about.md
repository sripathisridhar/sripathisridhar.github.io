I'm a PhD candidate in Information Systems at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), where I work in the [Sound Interaction and Computing (SInC) Lab](https://sinc-lab.com/) advised by [Dr. Mark Cartwright](https://markcartwright.com/). I expect to graduate in early 2027.

My research is in **machine listening** and **audio representation learning**. Recent work spans compositional audio representations, open-set audio classification, and audio-language models for sound design.

I've spent time as a research intern at the **Speech & Audio Group at Mitsubishi Electric Research Labs (MERL)** and in the **Sound Design AI Group at Adobe Research**. Before my PhD I completed a Master's in Music Technology at New York University and a Bachelor's in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at NIT Tiruchirappalli.

Outside of research, I'm a vocalist and drummer, and I've performed across ensembles and Indian classical music productions.

## Research interests
- Audio representation learning
- Audio-language models
- Machine listening and sound event detection
- Sound design and audio for interactive media
